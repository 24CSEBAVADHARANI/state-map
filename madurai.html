MADURAI

Madurai is a major city in Tamil Nadu, India, known as the cultural capital of the state ¹. It's situated on the banks of River Vaigai and has a rich history dating back over 2,500 years ¹. The city is closely associated with the Tamil language and was once the seat of the third Tamil Sangam, a congregation of Tamil scholars ¹. Demographics Madurai has a population of approximately 1,470,755 people, making it the third-largest metropolis in Tamil Nadu after Chennai and Coimbatore ¹. The city covers an area of 147.97 km² ¹. Assembly Constituencies Madurai has six assembly constituencies: - Melur - Madurai East - Madurai North - Madurai South - Madurai Central - Madurai West
